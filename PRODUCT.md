# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Indonesian travelers and professionals aged 25-50 who need reliable car rental services for business trips, family vacations, or airport transfers. They value transparency, convenience, and well-maintained vehicles. Booking context: mobile or desktop, comparing options, wanting quick confirmation.

## Product Purpose

JelajahCar is a car rental platform connecting travelers with quality vehicles across Indonesia. The landing page's job is to convert visitors into bookings by communicating trust, fleet quality, and ease of process. Success = completed booking form submission.

## Brand Personality

Reliable, modern, confident. Not flashy or luxury-obsessed, but premium enough to feel trustworthy. The brand speaks like a knowledgeable friend who knows the roads, not a corporate brochure.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS landing pages with identical 3-card feature grids
- Overly luxury/exclusive car rental sites that feel intimidating
- Budget rental sites with cluttered layouts and aggressive discount banners
- AI-generated cream/beige "warm" aesthetics

## Design Principles

- Transparency over persuasion: show real value, don't oversell
- Speed of booking: minimize friction from interest to confirmation
- Confident restraint: let the fleet speak, not decorative excess
- Indonesian-first voice: natural Bahasa Indonesia, not translated corporate speak
- Mobile-aware: most users browse on phone, book on desktop

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA contrast minimum. Reduced motion support. Indonesian language throughout. Date inputs with clear labels. Form validation with inline errors.
